About

I'm an Austin-based web designer/world traveler with aspirations of using creative technology for social good. By day, I work in the industry, pushing the edges of excellence and efficiency. I have this dream of using technology to make systems that work for people—improving access to information, fostering communication, empowering groups that might otherwise be voiceless. After a lifetime in the non-profit world, and a few years in the corporate one, I want to be a part of bridging the gap between the two.

My culturally and geographically confused background has taught me to love technology insofar as it grounds you and fosters human connection. I've lived in five countries on three continents and I still spend a great deal trying to strip away the superficial and expose whatever universal truths are underneath. I want my work to be a part of that.
